FIVE OUTSTANDING SENIOR SCOUTS

The search for the five outstanding senior scouts is on-going at Masbate National Comprehensive High School. The Five Outstanding Senior Scouts (FOSS) aim to recognize the hard-work, talent, and determination of Scout candidates at Outfit 002.

ABOUT

Every year starting this school year of 2026 - 2027, the Masbate National Comprehensive High School Senior Scouts aim to mold and develop and select five Scouts that have shown their determination to the organization, where they can serve and hold the title of "Outstanding Senior Scout" for one year, acting as a role model and example for future generations of Scouts.

SELECTION

To be an official candidate for the Five Outstanding Senior Scouts, a candidate application form must be submitted to the Screening Committee before the discussed deadline. FOSS is open to all Scouts with at least a year of experience in the organization.
